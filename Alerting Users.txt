Alerting Users
-simple updates:
	-Badges-> they are like labels,
eg. 
<h2 class="media-heading">
	Uthappizza
	<span class="badge badge-danger">Hot</span>
	<span class="badge badge--pill badge-secondary">$4.99</span>
</h2>

here, two labels will be created for the text uthappizza Hot and and the price $4.99, here badge danger and badge secondary are colours and badge-pill defines the shape of box of label, default shape is rectangle.

-Alert/Error/Warning messages
	-Alert
eg.
<div class="alert alert-warning alert-dismissible" role="alert">
	<button type="button" class="close" data-dismiss="alert">
		<span>&time;</span>
	</button>
	<strong>Warning:</strong>:Please
	<a href="tel:+85212345678" class="alert-link">
	call</a> us to reserve for more than sizx guests.
</div>

Here alert-warning is for the colour of the background of alert and alert-dismissible allows users to dismiss the alert.
<button> of class close is used to provide a button looking similar to x sign which allows users to dismiss the alert by clicking in it.

-Progress Bars
It shows the progress of a task initiated on the website.

eg.
<div classs="progress">
	<div class="progress-bar progress-bar-stiped bg-danger" role="progressbar" style="width:80%">
	80%
	</div>
</div>

Progress-bar-striped gives striped progress bar, we can also animate the progress bar.
